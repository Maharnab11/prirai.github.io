About

“The Fine Arts and Literary Council (FALC) aims at promoting the creative process, whether it be in the form of paintings, oration, literary quizzes, contests or visual arts. In this fast-paced society where college curricula often focus Science and Engineering, and students spend recreational time in front of the television, computer or video games, art education often gets overlooked. However, it is an essential part of a student's development.”

“It instills a sense of creativity in an individual and helps the person to gain a sense of accomplishment. FALC organizes regular events (competitions, creative sessions and seminars) to provide an ambiance of Fine Arts and Literary Activities in the Institute. Presently there are several clubs and activities running under its realm like fine arts club, oratory club, reader's club, movie club, literary club and Arts and Letters (Fine Arts and Literary Week).”
